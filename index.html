Welcome to Elementary!

...a highly unrealistic game about particle physics.

Drag particles from the sidebar on the right into the workspace where they may be combined to form new particles. Over time, new particles will be unlocked along with new workspaces.

The workspace is a timeless two-dimensional simulation of the universe associated with a specific force. You can change workspaces by using the arrows (provided others have been unlocked) or clear the current workspace with the clear icon. This message may be reprompted using the help icon.
